## Delivery notes
- Cut the stats when speaking — "a bunch of teams" sounds human; "30+ teams, 95% groundedness"
  sounds rehearsed. Numbers belong on the resume.
- Lead with identity (years + arc), end on what you actually enjoy — making AI genuinely
  useful and reliable.
- The throughline that ties it together: *"cool demo → trustworthy in production."*
